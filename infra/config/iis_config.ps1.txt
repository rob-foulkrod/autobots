For this demo to work, you will need to 
1) Open the Modules Tab in your Automation Account
2) Click 'Add Module'
3) Click 'Browse the gallery'
4) Click 'Browse from gallery' link
5) Paste 'xWebAdministration'
6) Click the xWebAdministration Module
7) Click Select button
8) Select the 5.1 in the Runtime version dropdown
